About The App

The Indian Sign Language learning application represents a significant step towards fostering a more inclusive society that values diversity and strives for equal opportunities for all. It hopes to ultimately contribute towards bridging the communication gap between individuals with hearing impairments and the wider hearing community. With its user-friendly design, interactive features, and personalized learning approach, we hope that the application serves as an effective platform for learners at various skill levels.
